Commissioning represents the final phase of validation and optimization of your installations. It includes testing, validation, training, and documentation delivery to guarantee compliance and performance of your equipment.

FAT (Factory Acceptance Test)

  • Performed at the factory before delivery to verify technical and contractual compliance.
  • Functional testing and operational validation.
  • Quality control and approval in the presence of the client.

SAT (Site Acceptance Test)

  • Performed on-site after full installation.
  • Verification of integration in the real environment.
  • Performance, safety, and reliability tests.
  • Final validation with the client.

  • First energization and start-up of installations.
  • Configuration and tuning of PLCs, drives, and HMI/SCADA interfaces.
  • Performance optimization and monitoring.
  • Training and support for client teams.
  • Preparation of a complete commissioning and validation report.

At the end of commissioning, GSA Automation provides all deliverables ensuring full traceability and compliance of the project:

  • Complete technical documentation (drawings, diagrams, BOMs, PLC programs).
  • Signed FAT/SAT protocols and reports.
  • Calibration and conformity certificates.
  • Testing reports and validation sheets.
  • User guides, operation manuals, and maintenance documentation.
