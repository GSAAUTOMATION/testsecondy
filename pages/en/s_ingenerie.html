At GSA Automation, we provide our clients with recognized expertise in engineering and technical studies, covering the entire design cycle of an industrial installation. Our goal is to ensure reliable, high-performance solutions tailored to the specific needs of each project, while complying with current safety standards and regulations.

Every project begins with a rigorous feasibility phase. We analyze your processes, technical constraints, and economic objectives to identify the most suitable solutions. This step provides you with a clear view of technical and financial possibilities before committing to investments.

We translate your operational needs into precise technical specifications. This functional analysis forms the solid foundation of the project, ensuring that every production, performance, or safety requirement is integrated from the start.

Precise equipment sizing is essential to ensure the performance and durability of your installations. Our engineers perform detailed calculations (power, torque, flow, speed, etc.) and select the most appropriate components: motors, drives, servodrives, sensors, actuators, measuring instruments, etc. Each choice is guided by reliability, energy efficiency, and compliance with industrial standards.

We design clear, standardized electrical schematics and P&IDs in compliance with international standards. These technical documents facilitate installation, reduce errors, and ensure safe and efficient maintenance of your equipment.

To optimize procurement and maintenance, we create a comprehensive, organized list of all components required for the project. This approach facilitates inventory management, reduces intervention time, and ensures full traceability of the equipment used.
