About GSA Automation

Who Are We?

GSA Automation is an engineering company specialized in industrial automation, electrical solution design, complex system integration, and the complete installation of industrial infrastructures, including special-purpose machines and production lines.

With expertise gained from major projects carried out across various industrial sectors, GSA Automation operates in Africa, Europe, and internationally, delivering performance, innovation, and reliability in every project.

Our Missions and Domains

Our missions cover the entire lifecycle of industrial installations, from technical requirements analysis to operational commissioning, including advanced process optimization.

We operate across the following areas:

Modernization and Retrofit of Industrial Systems

Upgrading automation architectures (DCS, PLC, SCADA, HMI...), replacing obsolete components, and migrating to more efficient and secure platforms.

Installation and Commissioning of Equipment and Infrastructure

Complete installation of special-purpose machines and production units, including the integration of electrical power and control panels, instrumentation, industrial processes, and piping networks.

We also handle compliance testing, FAT/SAT, and functional validation.

Process Optimization and Continuous Improvement

Analysis of performance, reduction of downtime, optimization of automatic sequences, calibration and reliability of instrumentation loops, and improvement of efficiency and equipment availability.

Safety and Regulatory Compliance

Implementation in compliance with IEC, ISO, NF, ATEX standards, functional analysis, risk management, and integration of safety systems (SIS, ESD, interlocks).

Technical Support, Maintenance, and Assistance

On-site and remote support, troubleshooting of anomalies, updating of PLC programs, preventive/predictive maintenance, and training of technical teams.
